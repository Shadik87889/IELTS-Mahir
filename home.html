Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our platform and services.

What makes IELTS Mahir different?

We stand out through our combination of expert-led instruction, personalized study plans, and advanced AI-powered tools like our Speaking Test Simulator. We focus on a holistic approach to help you achieve your best possible score.

Are the mock tests free?

We offer a free diagnostic test to help you understand your current level. Our comprehensive, full-length mock tests with detailed feedback are part of our premium packages to ensure you are fully prepared for the real exam.

Who are the instructors?

Our team consists of certified IELTS professionals with years of teaching experience. They have a proven track record of helping students achieve high band scores and are committed to your success.

How can I get a personalized study plan?

After taking our initial diagnostic test, our system and instructors will analyze your results to create a custom study plan tailored to your specific needs, focusing on the areas where you need the most improvement.

Is there a community forum?

Yes! We have a vibrant community forum where you can connect with other students, share tips, and get support from our mentors. It's a great place to stay motivated and engaged throughout your IELTS journey.
